**Lava Waders** combine the effects of the [Obsidian Water Walking Boots](@irregular_implements:obsidian_water_walking_boots) and the [Lava Charm](@irregular_implements:lava_charm), as well as allowing you to walk on Lava.

Get them by combining the two in an Anvil.
