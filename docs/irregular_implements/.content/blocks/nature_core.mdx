The **Nature Core** structure can be found randomly around the overworld, with a Nature Chest nearby full of goodies.

The Nature Core can also do these things randomly:
- Convert nearby Sand into Dirt/Grass
- Spawn an animal nearby
- Bone Meal nearby crops
- Plant Saplings nearby
- Repair the structure around it
